Instructions

  1. 1. Move & Transform

    Use Tritium functions such as move_here() to move other elements into the appropriate places.

    Transform these elements with functions such as add_class() and attribute().

    Remove unwanted elements with the remove() function.

  2. 2. Template & Merge

    For advanced use, try out the new template() function to give your page some quick structure and functionality.

    Fill in the template using the merge() and placeholder() functions.

    Learn more about how to use templates in the README.md file.

  3. 3. Adjust Styles

    Adjust the style gude to fit your liking. This includes the _variables.scss, _lib.scss, and _base.scss files.

    Adjust the page specific styles. This includes the header.scss, _footer.scss, and _home.scss files.
